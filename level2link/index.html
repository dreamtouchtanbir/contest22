FAQ

How Much Do You Charge for Security Services?

Our clients’ needs vary vastly, so the cost and scope of their security needs does too. What never changes is that our clients want the absolute best price every time.

When Linewatch provides a quote for your security needs, no matter how big or small you can trust us that we won’t cut corners, but we’ll make sure you’re getting the most competitive rate – first time, every time.

Is Linewatch a Registered Security Firm?

Yes. Linewatch is a registered Australian Security company under Australia’s private security act. The security firm is registered as Linewatch PTY LTD under the Private Security Business Licence number: 828-603-40S

How Do You Train Your Security Guards?

Linewatch security guards undergo extensive training and induction to ensure they remain across the latest industry best practices and they implement them in everything they do. This means that all of our employees are educated and trained to industry-best standards, and always behave according to Linewatch’s best practice Standard Operating Procedures.

Are Linewatch Guards Good with the Public?

At Linewatch, we only hire security agents who can prove they have strong inter-personal skills and emotional intelligence, and Managing Director Tolga Karabiyik, as an experienced industry trainer is a master at screening applicants for this.

We understand that when our agents are on the job, they are representing you, your company, your brand – so they are always ready to go the extra mile in making sure your patrons are happy, and assist in related areas such as traffic management

What's a Linewatch Security Assessment?

Linewatch security assessments involve a 360 degree audit of the premises or site of the event to identify all potential security risks in advance, and a plan to manage and minimise those risks.

Linewatch founder, Tolga Karabiyik is a fully qualified and experienced security industry trainer, and he always makes sure that Linewatch agents are skilled to perform security assessments according to industry best practices.

How Do You Operate With Police?

Linewatch agents are trained to operate in coordination with police and other official security agencies as required.

Under Australia’s Private Security Act 2010, Linewatch is under obligation to share certain information with police or other official security agencies upon request.
